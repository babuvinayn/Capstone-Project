About Us

All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) is a statutory body and a national-level council for technical education, under Department of Higher Education, Ministry of Education. Established in November 1945 as an advisory body, it was later given statutory status in 1987 by an Act of Parliament to regulate and coordinate technical education throughout India.

The council’s mission is to promote and develop quality technical education and management education in India in a coordinated and integrated manner. It is responsible for planning, formulation, and maintenance of norms and standards, accreditation of institutions, providing grants-in-aid, and monitoring and evaluation of the education system.

Our Vision

Promote quality, set high standards, and ensure excellence in technical education.

Our Mission

Develop policies, support institutions, and foster research in technical education.

Our Goals

Increase intake, improve quality, promote innovation and entrepreneurship.
