AICTE-Approved Research Project: Bio-Tech Innovations

This project focuses on developing sustainable bio-fuel from algae. It is an AICTE-approved initiative led by students and faculty from the Department of Biotechnology.

Project Highlights

  • **Institute:** National Institute of Technology, Bhopal
  • **Faculty Lead:** Dr. Ananya Sharma
  • **Status:** In Progress (Year 2 of 3)
  • **Objective:** To reduce dependency on fossil fuels by creating a cost-effective bio-fuel source.

Project Description

The research team has successfully identified a new strain of algae with a high lipid content. The next phase involves optimizing the cultivation process and testing the scalability of the bio-fuel production. The project has received an AICTE grant of ₹50 lakhs for equipment and research personnel.

Technologies Used
